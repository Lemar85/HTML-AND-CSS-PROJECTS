Teen Captain of The Juken Club Dies After Extreme Battle With Former Friend/Club Member!

After months of tension 19yo Shin Natsume, member and captain of the Juken Club and 18yo Mitsuomi Takayanagi a younger member training to become the next club captain, meet in an extreme battle to the death!
There has always been a mysterious energy surrounding the Juken Club but it seems as though tensions between the two members started to rise after Mitsuomi began a relationship with the club captain's beloved, younger sister.
This final battle came to an unfortunate end resulting in the death of Shin Natsume.
Where will the students and remaining club members go from here? Only time will tell as we all try to get through the aftermath of this unfortunate ending
